Flagship

Definition

1: the ship that carries the commander of a fleet or subdivision of a fleet and flies the commander's flag
2: the finest, largest, or most important one of a group of things (such as products, stores, etc.) —often used before another noun the company's flagship store The traveling media crew comprises seven beat writers and a reporter from WFAN, the team's flagship station and the granddaddy of all-sports radio. — Hank Hersch

Examples of flagship in a sentence

The flagship of the Atlantic fleet
